# Common Cryptographic Algorithms

## Symmetric
- AES (128/192/256-bit)
- ChaCha20
- 3DES (deprecated)

## Asymmetric
- RSA (2048/3072/4096-bit)
- ECC (Curve25519, secp256k1)
- Diffie-Hellman

## Hashing
- SHA-256, SHA-3
- MD5 (broken, avoid)
- SHA-1 (deprecated)

## Signatures
- RSA-PSS
- ECDSA
- EdDSA (Ed25519)
